Customer Service Scenarios

  1. Client complains driver is 10 min late, vehicle 500 m away. Best first response?
    A) Contact driver and call back.
    B) It’s nearby, please wait.
    C) I see it's close, I'll update driver and confirm shortly.
    D) Escalate immediately.
    Answer: C
    Rationale: acknowledges issue, shows monitoring, action + closes loop.
  2. Driver reports breakdown mid-trip with VIP onboard. Immediate priority?
    A) Notify client & advise alternative transport.
    B) Contact tech support then inform client.
    C) Confirm client safety/location, dispatch backup, notify client and ops.
    D) Wait for full report before action.
    Answer: C
    Rationale: focuses on safety, proactive resolution, escalation.
  3. Client can't request ride via app and needs urgent driver. Best response?
    A) Ask to restart or reinstall app first.
    B) Log issue for IT, ask them to wait.
    C) Dispatch driver manually, confirm with client, then report app issue.
    D) Apologize, send PDF instructions to retry later.
    Answer: C
    Rationale: resolves urgent need first, maintains service level, escalates tech issue.
